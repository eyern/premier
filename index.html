Services @Premier Neurosurgery

Brain Surgery

We perform complex procedures such as craniotomies, tumor resections, and surgeries for traumatic brain injuries.

Spinal Surgery

Minimally invasive techniques to treat herniated discs, fractures, and spinal cord compression.

Neurotrauma Services

Emergency care for traumatic injuries to the head, neck, and spine.

Post-Surgical Care

After surgery, patients receive comprehensive care that includes:
- Continuous monitoring in our specialized neuro-intensive care unit.
- Rehabilitation services to restore function and mobility.
- Pain management and emotional support for a smooth recovery.
- Family education and follow-up consultations to ensure long-term success.
